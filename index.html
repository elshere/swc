About Us

Startup World Cup is a global conference and competition that brings together the top startups, VCs, entrepreneurs and world-class tech CEOs. The Grand Finale brings all the regional champions to San Francisco where the winning team is awarded with a USD$1.000.000 investment prize. The goal of this event is to foster innovation and entrepreneurship worldwide.

Announcement

Startup World Cup is calling for those of you who have startups in Indonesia. Stasion (Komunitas Startup Malang), in collaboration with the Startup World Cup, is looking for potential startups to become Indonesian champions in the form of a pitch competition. A very exciting opportunity to compete with global startup champions in Silicon Valley. With the chance to win an investment of USD $1,000,000, you can also get the opportunity to find new connections in a global network of industry experts, gain invaluable insights of business and find your way to success. Join and become the Startup World Cup champion, leaving your name on this world stage.
